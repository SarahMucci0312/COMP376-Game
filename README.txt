# Debunked

//Story
Following the disappearance of his wife, detective Bean receives a strange message leading him to an
abandoned bunker on the outskirst of town. Upon entry, he finds his wife restrained at the center of the 
room by an old foe. If he and his wife want to leave the bunker alive, they'll first have to solve the games the
kidnapper set up for them and ultimately, the mystery of their identity.

//Controls
WASD: Movement
E: Interact with buttons
Enter: Go to next dialogue
Escape: Pause menu
Tab: Open/Close password puzzle
